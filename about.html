Our Story

Pawsitive Care was founded on a simple belief: dogs deserve a calm, compassionate environment where caregivers treat them with respect and gentleness. We are a team of professional dog lovers, veterinarians and certified trainers dedicated to enriching the lives of dogs and peace of mind for their guardians. Our approach blends evidence-based training with enrichment, nutrition guidance, and preventive wellness, all tailored to the unique temperament of each dog. We work closely with families to create consistent routines that support happiness, safety, and trust. over the years, we have built a community built on transparency, reliability, and a shared passion for canine well-being.

Our facilities are designed to minimize stress: quiet handling rooms, softly lit grooming areas, and climate-controlled play spaces.
